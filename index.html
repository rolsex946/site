Doctor Profile

Dedicated to Your Well-Being

Dr. Asmita Mondal is a highly qualified General Physician and Diabetologist practicing in Kolkata, West Bengal. With a strong foundation in modern medicine from West Bengal University of Health Sciences (WBUHS) and specialized certification in Diabetes management (CCEBDM), she brings a comprehensive approach to patient care.

Her tenure as House Physician in the Department of Emergency Medicine at R.G. Kar Medical College & Hospital — one of the premier medical institutions in Eastern India — equipped her with extensive clinical experience in managing acute and chronic medical conditions under high-pressure environments.

Driven by a commitment to continuous learning, Dr. Mondal earned certification from Harvard Medical School's International Respiratory Diseases Programme, bringing globally recognized expertise to her respiratory care practice in Kolkata.

Dr. Asmita Mondal holds an MBBS degree from West Bengal University of Health Sciences (WBUHS) and a CCEBDM certification in Diabetes Management. She is also certified by Harvard Medical School in their International Respiratory Diseases Programme. She is registered with the West Bengal Medical Council (Reg. No. 86487).

Dr. Mondal treats a wide range of conditions including Diabetes (Type 1, Type 2, Gestational), Hypertension (Blood Pressure), Thyroid Disorders (Hypothyroidism, Hyperthyroidism), Respiratory Issues (Asthma, COPD, Respiratory Infections), and General Medicine conditions like Fever, Cough/Cold, Stomach Disorders, Pain-related issues, and other general illnesses.
